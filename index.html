About the Program

The VCU x M.A.D Science Internship Program is a joint venture research initiative designed to deliver real-world bioinformatics project. Students gain experience working with genomic data, machine learning models, and scientific storytelling.

Research Areas

Genomics

DNA sequencing, variant analysis, and gene discovery.

Machine Learning

Predictive modeling for biological datasets.

Systems Biology

Understanding complex biological interactions.
